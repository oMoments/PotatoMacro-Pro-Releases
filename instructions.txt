===========================================
  POTATO MACRO PRO - INSTRUCTIONS
===========================================

GETTING STARTED
---------------
1. Make sure AutoHotkey v2 is installed.
2. Double-click PotatoLauncher_Pro.ahk to open the launcher.
3. Log in with your username and password.
4. Your Roblox window will appear in the list automatically.
5. Go to the Settings tab and fill in your coordinates, then click Save Settings.
6. Come back to Main, select your window, and press Start (or F4).


SETTINGS TAB - WHAT EVERYTHING MEANS
--------------------------------------

Resolution
  Set this to your Roblox window size. Click Auto-detect to fill it in automatically.

Sell
  Golden Potatoes Tab  - the golden potatoes tab button in the sell screen.
  Sell All Button      - the sell all button.

Generators
  Buy Button (column X) - the X position of any green buy button in the generators list.
  Y Top                 - the Y position of the highest visible buy button.
  Y Bot                 - the Y position of the lowest visible buy button (should be half peeking in).
  Row Spacing           - the pixel distance between two buy buttons. Use the + picker to measure.
  Scroll Area           - anywhere inside the generator scroll list.

Prestige
  Prestige Now    - the Prestige Now button.
  Prestige Confirm - the confirm button on the prestige popup.

Ascend Coords (only needed if using Ascend mode)
  Blessing of Abundance - the Abundance path button.
  Blessing of Prestige  - the Prestige path button.
  Ascend Confirm        - the confirm button on the ascend popup.

Inventory Swap (optional)
  Equip the best potato for prestige right before prestiging,
  then swap back to your potato bonus potato after.
  Prestige Potato  - click the potato that buffs prestige points.
  Equip (prestige) - its equip button.
  Bonus Potato     - click the potato that buffs potato gain.
  Equip (bonus)    - its equip button.
  Leave all four as 0 to disable this feature.

Shop (optional)
  The macro uses 8 explicit BUY button coordinates — one per shop slot.
  IMPORTANT: Your shop must be arranged in 2 columns of 4 rows each.
    Left column, top to bottom  = Btn 1, 2, 3, 4
    Right column, top to bottom = Btn 5, 6, 7, 8
  Set each Btn coordinate to the exact BUY button for that slot.
  Leave a slot as 0 to skip it. Leave all as 0 to disable the shop entirely.
  To actually enable auto-buying, check "Auto Shop (every 5 min)" on the Main tab.

  Skip Rock / Useless Rock - when enabled on the Main tab, the macro will
                   detect and skip rock items using image recognition.

Rock Images (for Skip Rocks feature)
  The macro uses two image files to detect rocks:
    rock.png
    useless_rock.png
  These must be in the same folder as the launcher.
  You can use your own cropped screenshots of the rock icons.
  Just name them correctly and drop them in the folder.

  Rock Box - defines where the rock icon appears relative to each BUY button.
  Use the "Set" button in Settings to set it automatically:
    1. Click any BUY button in the shop (anchor).
    2. Click the top-left corner of the rock icon image.
    3. Click the bottom-right corner of the rock icon image.
  Off X / Off Y is the offset from the buy button to the rock icon.
  W / H is the size of the search area.

Keybinds
  You can change the Start, Stop All, and Bring to Front hotkeys to whatever you want.
  Click Set next to each one and press your desired key.


MAIN TAB
--------
  Auto-Find    - detects your Roblox window size automatically.
  Start        - starts the macro on the selected window.
  Stop All     - stops all running macros.
  Ascend            - enable this if you want the macro to ascend after each prestige.
                      Choose your ascend path (Blessing of Abundance or Blessing of Prestige).
  Inventory Swap    - enable this to swap to your prestige potato before prestiging,
                      then swap back to your bonus potato afterwards.
  Auto Shop         - enable this to have the macro open the shop every 5 minutes and buy
                      everything available. Requires shop coordinates to be set in Settings.


HOW THE LOOP WORKS
-------------------
Each loop the macro does the following:
  1. Sells golden potatoes.
  2. Buys generators (repeats several times per loop).
  3. Waits for the right moment then sells again and prestiging.
  4. If Inventory Swap is set up, equips the prestige potato before prestiging
     and swaps back to the bonus potato after.
  5. If Ascend is enabled, ascends after the prestige.
  6. Every 5 minutes, opens the shop and buys available items
     (only if Shop coords are set and Auto Shop is checked on the Main tab).


UPDATES
-------
  When a new version is available you will see a blue Update button at the top of the launcher.
  Click it and the update downloads and restarts automatically.
  Your settings and coordinates are saved and will not be affected by updates.


COORDINATE PICKER
-----------------
  Every coordinate has a + button next to it.
  Click + and the launcher hides itself.
  Then click the exact spot in your Roblox window.
  The coordinates are filled in automatically.


TIPS
----
  - Always click Save Settings after making any changes.
  - The macro uses color detection to find green buy buttons, so make sure
    your game is visible and not covered by other windows while running.
  - If the macro stops buying generators, double-check your Y Bot coordinate
    is set to a buy button that is half visible at the bottom of the list.
  - For the shop, make sure your items are arranged in 2 columns before
    setting coordinates. The macro clicks each button directly — no scrolling.
